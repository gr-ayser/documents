# Privacy Policy for Financely **Last Updated:** March 2026 ## 1. Introduction Welcome to Financely. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application. ## 2. Data We Collect Financely is designed to be privacy-first. * **Financial Data:** All your transactions, goals, and categories are stored locally in an SQLite database on your device. We do not transmit or store this financial data on our servers. * **Authentication Data:** We use Google Sign-In to authenticate you. This provides us with basic profile information such as your email address, name, and profile picture, strictly for displaying within the app and managing your Backup & Sync preferences. * **Google Drive Data:** If you enable the "Backup & Sync" feature, the app will upload an encrypted copy of your local database to a hidden, app-specific folder (`appDataFolder`) in your personal Google Drive account. We do not have access to your other Google Drive files, and we do not store your backup on our own servers. * **Advertising Data:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect device identifiers, location data (if permitted by the device), and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. ## 3. How We Use Your Data * To provide and maintain the application's core functions (tracking income/expenses). * To enable cloud backups securely to your own Google Drive. * To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob, which helps keep the app free. ## 4. Third-Party Services We employ the following third-party companies and services: * **Google Play Services:** For core Android functionality. * **Google Sign-In & Google Drive API:** For authentication and backup functionality. * **Google AdMob:** For advertisement delivery. These third parties have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose. Please review Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more details. ## 5. User Rights & Data Deletion Because your financial data is stored locally, you maintain complete control over it. You can delete all your financial data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's localized data in your device settings. If you wish to delete your Google Drive backups, you can do so by managing the hidden app data via your Google Drive settings on the web. ## 6.
